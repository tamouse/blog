A small snippet showing how to use the #downto method.

The Beer Song
  1. def beersong(bb=99)
  2.  
  3.   bb.downto(1) do |n|
  4.  
  5.     puts <<SONG
  6. #{n} bottles of beer on the wall,
  7. #{n} bottles of beer!
  8. Take one down and pass it around,
  9. #{n>1 ? n-1 : "No more"} bottles of beer on the wall!"
  10.  
  11. SONG
  12.  
  13.   end
  14.  
  15.   puts " .. they all fell down drunk!"
  16.  
  17. end
