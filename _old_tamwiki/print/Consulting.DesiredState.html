The goal of PrecisionInquiry, the Desired State is a description of the client's desired future, how they would like things to be.

It is typically derived by gathering WellFormedOutcomes for the future. One's future desired state is very powerful for people to have; it provides a target for them to move towards, gives purpose for change and helps them determine what they need to know, acquire and do in order to get that desired future. The ThreeGoldenQuestions are often used to create a rich desired state for the client.
