Rational Simplicity: Setting Course to a Simpler Life by Tim Cowell is a book about getting down to the basics, and doing what you want in life, and only working how much you need to in order to enjoy your life. It is a book about changing values, from consumerism to simplicity. Here are the basic tenets:

  • Separate want from need
  • Think of money as time
  • Break the advertising chain
  • Avoid consumer debt
  • Know your net worth
  • Understand the magic of compound interest
  • Set goals
  • Choose a path to simplicity
  • Pursue your dream
