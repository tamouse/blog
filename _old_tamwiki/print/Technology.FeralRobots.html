In an article by Cory Doctorow at Boing-Boing, he writes about a talk at the 2005 O'Reilly Emerging Technology Conference (ETECH) containing the idea of Feral Robots: taking toy robotic dogs and mod-ing them so they perform useful functions.

The ETECH talk by Natalie Jeremijenko is: Social Robotics, Scmocial Robotics: Feral Robotics and Some Other Quacking, Shaking, Bubbling (what would the opposite of feral be?) Robots
