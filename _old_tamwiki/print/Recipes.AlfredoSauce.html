This starts with your basic WhiteSauce and adds parmesan. Couldn't be easier, really!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of WhiteSauce
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Make the WhiteSauce according to the directions
  2. Before the milk mixture thickens, add the parmesan cheese. Stir until desired thickness/texture emerges.

Comments

This is the basic sauce I use for Tamara's Tetrazzini.
