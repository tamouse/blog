From: Andrea Novak

Ingredients:

  • 1 pot roast (approximately 5-6 lbs.)
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 3 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 packet onion soup mix

Directions

  1. Place roast, water, boullion cubes and soup mix into a crockpot. Cook on high for 7-8 hours. Take roast out and flake. Put juices into a separate bowl and place in freezer for 1 hour. Take out and skim fat off of the top. Place meat and juice back in crockpot and cook for another 3 hours.
