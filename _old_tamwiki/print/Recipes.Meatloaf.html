Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons malt-o-meal
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • fresh ground pepper

Glaze

  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard

Directions

  1. mix gently with as few strokes as possible to combine everything
  2. fill a bread loaf pain
  3. bake 45 minutes at 350??F
  4. mix together glaze
  5. drain fat from pan, coat meatloaf with glaze, bake 20 minutes longer at 350??F

Makes 6 servings.

Comments

Baked potatoes cook at a higher temperature than the meatloaf does, so if you cook them together, you need to cook the potatoes longer.
