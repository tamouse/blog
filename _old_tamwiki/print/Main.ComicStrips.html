Topical

  • xkcd - A webcomic of romance, sarcasm, math, and language. Randall Monroe's epic stick figure web comic. This is truly the best on the web.
  • Saturday morning breakfast cereal -- Zack Weiner's classic covering science, beliefs, politicsl, human behaviour, and skepticism occasionally NSFW
  • Calamities of Nature -- a bunch of animals discuss heady topics of science, politics, human behaviour, and skepticism -- gone :(
  • Tree Lobsters -- you can't prove they don't exist! makes fun of religion, anti-science
  • Jesus and Mo - a comic about Jesus and Mohammed, frequently showing how silly religion is
  • Surviving the World -- not really a comic, it's a photograph of a guy at a blackboard with the joke written out. Quite funny.
  • A Softer World -- quirky, photo-based, web comic that isn't really a comic so much as a running commentary on things. Objects in web page are deeper than they appear.

  • User Friendly -- life in tech support
