Description
Temple Bar is a unique music venue and meeting place; a cultural crossroads where patrons unwind amidst the vibes created by a community of local, national and international artists. The "Eastern-meets-Urban" theme provides a relaxed groove as edgy hip-hop rhythms and rich funky bass lines pierce the incense-laden air, and the omnipresent Buddha smiles knowingly down upon the crowd. Those desiring earthly pleasures indulge in spicy food and stimulating drinks, while others seeking a higher connection give themselves over to serious boogie on the dance floor.
