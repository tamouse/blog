Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled
  • 6 tablespoons tequila
  • 2 tablespoons triple sec
  • 1/4 cup limeade concentrate
  • 2 cups crushed ice

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in blender
  2. Process on high until frothy
  3. Pour into margerita glasses
