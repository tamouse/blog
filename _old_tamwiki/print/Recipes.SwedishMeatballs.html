Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Ingabretson's Swedish meatball mix1
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • handfull of malt-o-meal

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350℉.
  2. mix together meat, egg, milk, and malt-o-meal well.2
  3. make one inch balls and arrange in baking pan
  4. bake for 30 minutes at 350.
