"Hello"/"Greetings"
-------------------

Thailand
:   "sawadee ka" (if you're female)\
    "sawadee krap" (if you're male)

Hindi
:   "namaste" (literally, "I greet and acknowledge the spirit within
    you")

Strine
:   "G'day, Mate!"
