Ingredients
-----------

-   1/2 pound bacon
-   1/2 pound ground beef
-   1 cup chopped onion
-   1/2 cup ketchup
-   1/2 cup brown sugar
-   1/4 cup granulated sugar
-   1 teaspoon salt
-   2 tablespoons vinegar
-   1 teaspoon dry mustard
-   1 16 ounce can baked beans
-   1 16 ounce can red kidney beans
-   1 16 ounce can lima or butter beans
