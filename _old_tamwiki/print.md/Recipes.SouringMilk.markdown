An alternative to using buttermilk.

<span id="ingredients"></span>

Ingredients
-----------

-   1 cup of sweet milk
-   1 1/3 tablespoons white vinegar

<span id="directions"></span>

Directions
----------

1.  Add the vinegar to the milk and set aside for 15 minutes before
    using.
