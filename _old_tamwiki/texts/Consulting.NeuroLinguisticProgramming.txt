Neuro-Linguistic Programming is a set of techniques, skills and practices that allow a practitioner to be able to guide and direct a client to a resourceful state in which they are able to acheive their goals and desired state.

[[Wikipedia(:Neuro-linguistic_programming)]] provides a more in-depth overview of what NLP is.
