Shout Out for Codeship.IO: Adding Transcripts to Video Blogs

Aug 8, 2013

In which your humble correspondent sings the praises for some people really trying to get it.

The folks over at CodeshipIO run a weekly series called “Testing Tuesday” that I really enjoy. It is a video lesson, which I often have a hard time following due to a bit of hearing loss and just the way I process things. I left a comment asking if they could do transcripts, not really expecting much because it’s just these guys with their business, ya know?

Well, I got back an enthusiastic reply and they’ve agreed to start putting up transcripts!

Well done, Codeship!

There’s some improvements to be made, as with anything, and I hope they’ll be able to keep it going alongside the videos. The series itself is awesome and I highly recommend it to people who want to get their game on in continuous testing.

Again, thank you, Codeship, very well done!
