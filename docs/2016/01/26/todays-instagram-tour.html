Jellies at MBA

Anyone have peanut butter for that jelly? Jellies are more than just pretty to look at—they're heavyweights of the ocean ecosystem. Plentiful and potent, they're powerful planktonic predators, as well as prey for numerous species—perhaps even an anemone!
