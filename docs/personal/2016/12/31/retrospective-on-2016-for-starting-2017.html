Retrospective on 2016 for starting 2017

Dec 31, 2016

So, it’s New Year’s Eve 2016. A traditional time for reflection. The following are not resolutions, however. Taking my queue from Agile retrospectives, I’ve written the “Do More Of, Do Less Of, and Keep the Same”:

Things I want to do more of in 2017:

  • write / journal more
  • draw / sketch more
  • paint outside of painting class
  • document my learnings more often
  • organize these things and make them habitual
  • walk

Things I want to do less of in 2017:

  • eating carbohydrates
  • hoarding cardboard boxes
  • social media

Things I’m planning to keep about the same in 2017:

  • reading
  • learning
  • practicing
