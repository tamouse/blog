I am not my memories...
... I am remembering

I am not my thoughts...
... I am thinking

I am not my emotions...
... I am feeling

I am not my things...
... I am making

I am living, constantly changing
Change is the lifeforce
All else flows by
From if to now to then

I make
I feel
I think
I remember

I am now
