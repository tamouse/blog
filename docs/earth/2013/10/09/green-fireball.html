Another awesome photo from Randy Halverson of Dakotalapse showing a green fireball – the streak on the left side of the page.

Randy’s words:

Green Fireball - I saw the green fireball with my eyes (green trail on the left), shortly after I started this timelapse. It moved from left to right and lasted several seconds. In Buffalo Gap National Grassland — Randy Halverson, Dakotalapse

I so love Randy’s work. His epic “Horizons” film fills me with wonder and joy. I just can’t get enough of his stuff.
