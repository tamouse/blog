Don't Judge a House by It's Exterior 09 Mar 2014

  • strange

This 4 bedroom property resides in Hillingdon, Middlesex in the UK. It is anything short of your average home.

From the outside, this home looks very typical and reserved.

Here’s a view of the backyard: very cute and homely feeling. There’s even a swing set.

You step in and realize everything is colored purple, including walls, carpets, and furniture.

Kitchen too.

Even the bathroom did not escape this shade.

The asking price of this house is 400,000 British pounds. Hopefully with this kind of interior decor, prospective buyers can negotiate for a steep discount, or maybe the seller is lucky to find a buyer with the same love for violet.
