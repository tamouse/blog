DeviantArtist TsaoShin (aka Eric Proctor) has won the Deviousness Award for his wonderful mashups of Disney movies and the Internet’s most famous grumpy cat.

TsaoShin’s Grumpy Disney Gallery is full of great images he’s created with one simple rule:

“The appearance of the cat must derail the plot of the film.”

This is really so awesome, and he’s quite talented.

Head on over and check them all out. His other work is fabulous as well. Well-deserved award!
